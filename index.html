About Our Project

Our website is a public feedback and complaint system designed to improve our city and help the community speak up about local problems. Many people face issues such as broken roads, damaged streetlights, overflowing garbage, water leaks, traffic problems, and unsafe public areas, but they often do not know where or how to report them. Our platform provides a simple and organized way for citizens to share these problems with the responsible authorities.
